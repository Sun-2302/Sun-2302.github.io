More About Me

My fullname is RAFANOMEZANTSOA Dera Miaro Daniel. I am 30 years old and I have been drawing for about 15 years.

Besides drawing, I also love crocheting, pottery and photography.

I am part of the association called Progress Art Work. We organize several workshops and exhibitions during the year.

Mainty sy Fotsy

02 February 2024

This exhibition features a selection of artworks that explore the use of black and white in different forms of artistic expression.
As you enter the gallery, you will immediately be struck by the variety of works on display, with ink paintings, charcoal drawings and woodblock prints presented side by side, creating a captivating juxtaposition of artworks from different eras and styles.
Some works play on the extreme contrasts of black and white to create dramatic and arresting effects. Others use subtle shades of gray to create a range of textures and tones.

Lanitra

14 November 2025

The exhibition entitled "Sky" invites visitors to contemplate and explore different artistic interpretations of this fascinating natural phenomenon. This exhibition offers a variety of perspectives on the sky, ranging from realistic depictions to more abstract and conceptual interpretations.
The artworks on display cover a wide range of styles and mediums. Some pieces may capture the vastness and majestic beauty of the sky, while others may focus on the texture, color or light of the sky.

Fitiavana

25 August 2026

This exhibition features a collection of artworks that celebrate the timeless theme of love in all its forms. This exhibition brings together a variety of pieces from different eras and cultures that explore the many dimensions of love, whether it be romantic, familial, friendly or even self-love.
Upon entering the gallery, you will immediately be immersed in a warm and comforting atmosphere. The walls are decorated with bright and vibrant paintings of passionate love scenes.
